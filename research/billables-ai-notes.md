# Billables.ai — Competitive Research

Research conducted May 23, 2026 for Bilang feature inspiration.

## Core Differentiator
AI-powered passive time capture (vs. Bilang's manual start/stop). Targets lawyers/consultants at $47-169/user/month.

## Top Ideas for Bilang (ranked by impact for PH freelancers)

1. **Matters/Sub-Projects** — sessions organized under client → project hierarchy
2. **Invoice-Ready Report** — clean printable PDF with billable sessions per client
3. **Google Calendar Sync** — auto-fill session descriptions from calendar events
4. **Idle Detection + Auto-Pause** — pause timer after inactivity, ask to resume or discard
5. **Analytics Dashboard** — hours/client, earnings trends, busiest days/times, utilization rate

## Bilang Advantage
Being **free and offline** is already a massive differentiator vs. cloud tools. Key marketing angle: "100% offline, no tracking, your data never leaves your device."
